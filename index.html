Dreameland

Dreameland is a big project in the NFT Game market with the classic farming game model. With brilliant graphic designs, vivid colours and creative thinking involved, this will be a NFT game that you cannot miss. You will act as a farmer, with a land and farm of your own, where you can plant trees, raise animals and build your farm.

Of action
Play and earn

Not only play-to-earn like other NFT Games, DreameLand focuses on the fact that players can build their own system and develop it from there. You will own your land, and from that LAND, you can build, produce and make in-game social contacts to earn profit.

Play together

Besides, players can also make contact with other players via social features. All those in-game contacts will help you receive valuable rewards.

Experience friendly game graphics

Focusing on upgrading the UI/UX for players, with 3D low poly graphic design, DreameLand intend to bring players more traditional and devices-friendly platform.

Game play
Game play
Game play
Players will own a land, then direct resources for reclamation and build that land into farms, animal lodging and fruit gardens. In the harvest seasons, players will earn the basic materials and move them to in-game factories in order to make the end products. These products will then be traded on the Marketplace for profit.
